This scatter plot shows the correlation between cloud cover and latitudes. There are roughly 600 latitude/longitude combinations plotted.

The x-axis is latitude, and the y-axis is cloudiness. Oddly, at the time of the data retrieval, there were more clouds in the sky where the latitudes are in increments of ten.
